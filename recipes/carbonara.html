Carbonara

Ingredients

  • Egg Yolk
  • pepper
  • Linguini
  • parmasan
  • guancale

Steps

  1. Crack and split egg yolk from whites into a bowl
  2. Boil Water and add linguini, reduce to simmer for 12 mins
  3. Grate parmasan and mix with egg yolks, add in cracked black pepper
  4. Dice quancale and cook on medium heat until golden/crispy
  5. Add pasta to pan with quancale and remove from heat
  6. Add the mixture into pan with pasta and quancale and stir
  7. add a dash of pasta water and give final mix
  8. Serve
